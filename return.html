Return Policy

At ZENYAR, we stand behind the quality of every product. If you are not completely satisfied, we are here to help.

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached
  • Sale items and customized products are not eligible for return
  • Original packaging must be intact

Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the item
  • Refund will be issued via the original payment method
  • For COD orders, refund will be sent via Easypaisa or bank transfer
  • Delivery charges are non-refundable unless the item is defective
